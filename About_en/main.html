Taiwan as a Strategic Location for Quantum Technologies

Taiwan offers a practical environment for the development and deployment of quantum technologies, supported by a mature semiconductor industry and strong research capabilities:

  • Active research and industrial community, with universities, national labs, ITRI and corporate R&D teams looking for concrete quantum software, hardware and QKD solutions to integrate into their roadmaps.

  • Deep semiconductor and manufacturing ecosystem, providing a natural fit for QPUs across platforms, cryogenic and vacuum systems, control electronics, interconnects and precision components.

  • Structured national investment in quantum, advanced chips, AI and related infrastructure, creating sustained demand for enabling technologies and platforms.
